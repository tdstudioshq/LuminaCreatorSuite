## Route Map Alignment

The application route map has been aligned to `docs/MVP_ROUTE_AUDIT_CHECKLIST.md`.

either redirected to the closest MVP route or removed when they were unrelated client/demo
microsites. Newly added MVP shells are intentionally frontend-only placeholders until backend,
payments, compliance, and launch workflows are implemented.
